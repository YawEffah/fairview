Who We Are

Fairview Clinic, founded in March 2014, is a trusted healthcare provider in Asufufu, Sunyani - Bono Region, Ghana. Under the leadership of Dr. William Okrah Konadu, a dedicated Family Physician Specialist, we have nearly a decade of experience delivering compassionate and comprehensive medical care.
Our clinic is committed to providing exceptional healthcare services to individuals and families in our community.

Fairview Clinic is dedicated to promoting health and wellness beyond our facility by offering free community outreaches and health screening services. Through these initiatives, we aim to raise awareness about preventive care, detect health issues early, and ensure that underserved communities have access to essential healthcare services.
